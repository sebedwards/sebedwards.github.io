Terms

How we charge

  • Hourly Rate for small jobs.

  • Daily Rate (8 hrs).

  • Cost of consumables (small quantities of screws / nails / materials / sealers etc) included in the basic hourly / daily rate.

  • No VAT charges on labour and general consumables.

  • No Call out charges.

  • Materials charged at cost (including vat) or can be charged to customer accounts or materials can be supplied by the customer.

  • If Tool Hire is necessary this will be charged at cost price.

  • General Fuel costs (grass / hedge cutting etc) included in the hourly rate.

  • Prolonged use of diesel / petrol machines (e.g. 8 hr running times) may result in additional fuel charges, customers however will be advised prior to commencing work.

Payment/Invoicing

  • Task invoices are supplied.

  • Payment - Cash / Cheque / BACS (On line bank transfers).

  • Payment Due - On completion of task or by prior arrangement (e.g. stage payments).

  • Late payment may result in additional fees to cover loss of interest etc. To avoid embarrassment, customers are respectfully requested to contact me immediately if they envisage a delay in payment.

Job estimates (free of charge)

  • Small tasks are normally charged by the hour, however unforeseen obstacles / problems can arise, in such cases customers will be informed of the problems arising and of any additional cost.

  • Detailed job estimates can be provided and remain valid for 30 days.

Health + Safety information

  • Tony Do it All Handyman Services is covered by Public Liability insurance (copies can be provided on request).

  • Tony holds an IOSH (Institute of Occupational Safety and Health) Qualification in "Managing Safely". Covering HSE Risk Management, Risk Assessment, COSHH assessment across a broad spectrum of work relate scenarios.

  • All tasks are fully risk assessed, dynamic risk assessment for small jobs, and documented risk assessment / safe system of work for larger tasks.

  • All company owned tools and equipment are fit for purpose, maintained and inspected on a regular / daily basis.

  • All hire equipment is supplied with hire company test certificates.

  • Material (COSHH) Data Sheets can be provided on request.
